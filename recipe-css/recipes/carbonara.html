Carbonara

Description

A super rich "bacon and egg" pasta dish that's great to serve for company. This recipe also makes an unusual brunch offering.

Follow this stripped back guide or visit AllRecipes for the full details.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Olive Oil
  • Diced Bacon
  • Onion
  • Dry White Wine
  • Large Eggs
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Fresh Parsley

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite.
  2. Place diced bacon in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir until evenly browned.
  3. Cook chopped onion in bacon fat until translucent; add garlic, cook till fragrant; add wine, cook for 1 min.
  4. Return cooked bacon to skillet, add spaghetti and beaten eggs, toss constantly until eggs are barely set.
  5. Add parmesan and season remembering cheese is salty.
